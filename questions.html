How Does Eatify Works

  • Where can we deliver orders?

    For this first phase, we can only provide pick up and drop off services in the India. Eatify plans to expand and get ready for the areas we will choose after India.

  • Does Eatify serve very large orders (more than 150 boxes)?

    Eatify adapts to the capacity of Chefs. Chefs can revise the dates and times ordered to adjust capacity such as preparing the ingredients, tools and labor they need to work on the orders ordered. If you want to order a very large quantity, make sure you order it long before the big day (min. H-5).

Pricing

  • Does the price listed for each product include shipping costs?

    Not yet. Our system will adjust the shipping costs based on the distance from the selected Chef's kitchen to the destination location you selected, which is added to the total price of the products you ordered.

  • How much is the shipping cost for every order from Eatify?

    Our system will adjust the delivery costs based on the distance from the selected Chef's kitchen to the destination location you selected.

  • What are the shipping costs if I order from 2 different Chefs?

    Our system will still calculate 2 different transactions with 2 different shipping costs.

Ordering

  • What is meant by Minimum Order?

    The smallest quantity determined by each Chef that a Buyer can order, and each product has a different minimum order.

  • Can I order for same day delivery?

    No. Orders ordered today (maximum limit until payment at 14.00), will be received the next day at the earliest at 09.30 and at the latest at 18.30. If after 14.00, the order will be delivered the next 2 days. If the Chef feels that the time you ordered is not enough to prepare the ingredients, tools and labor, the Chef can change the Request Delivery Time of your order, and you will be notified of the change.

  • What is the delivery time at Eatify?

    You can choose a Request Delivery Time, namely 09.30, 11.30, 13.30, 15.30, 18.30

  • Can delivery be made outside of the available hours?

    No. Due to limited chemical capacity, we schedule deliveries at 09.30, 11.30, 13.30, 15.30, 18.30

  • What is Pick Up Time and Delivery Time?

    Pick Up Time is the time/hour when the Server (deliverer) arrives at the Chef's kitchen location. Delivery Time is the time/hour when the order arrives at the Buyer's location where it has been ordered.

  • What is Pick Up Time and Delivery Time?

    Pick Up Time is the time/hour when the Server (deliverer) arrives at the Chef's kitchen location. Delivery Time is the time/hour when the order arrives at the Buyer's location where it has been ordered.

  • Can I order from more than 1 different Chef at the same time?

    Yes. Our system will still calculate 2 or more different transactions, with 2 or more different shipping costs.

  • Can I change or cancel an order I have placed?

    Yes, if your order status is still Open Request. No, if you have already made payment.

  • What if the order that arrives does not match what I ordered?

    Immediately contact us here as soon as you can.

  • What if I have allergies or don't like certain food/drink ingredients?

    You can mention this in the Special Instructions and the Chef will pay special attention when they prepare your order.

  • Can I pick up my order myself?

    No. Eatify has committed and set up a Kimi system to deliver every order that comes in through Eatify.

  • What if my order doesn't arrive?

    Immediately contact us here as soon as you can.

Payments

  • Do we accept cash payments or COD (Cash On Delivery)?

    No. Currently we only accept payments via (BCA) Bank Transfer.

  • Do we accept payment via credit card?

    No. Currently we only accept payments via (BCA) Bank Transfer.

Account Management

  • How do I create a Buyer account at Eatify?

    You can create an account by visiting backtoeatify.com and using your email to sign up. You will enter your cellphone number and password, after that you can immediately add an order and schedule the delivery you want.

  • Can Buyers join as Chef Eatify too?

    Yes. You can contact us directly via info@backtoEatify.com

Suggestion & Feedback

  • What if I have criticism and suggestions?

    Your opinion is very valuable to us. If you have criticism and suggestions please contact us here. We will immediately review it precisely and quickly.

  • Can I recommend a Chef or Shop for Eatify?

    Yes. We are always happy to add Chefs and make your favorites available on Eatify. Please contact us here with your recommended Chef and we will do the best we can to make it a reality.
