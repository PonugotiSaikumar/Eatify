Eatify is a technology program that connects your home food & beverage business with the community, and helps develop it.
Why Eatify

You create & innovate with your menu, we handle transactions and delivery.

Eatify will make your transactions easy, help promote you and your products, and manage order delivery. We will continue to test and learn together to increase sales volume, maintain smooth operations, and impress your customers.

How Eatify Works

Sign up Now

Sign Up now at backtoeatify.com, (Contact us to open a Channel (shop). Look at Contact Us). Then upload your cool logo, photos of your products along with prices, min. order, product description, and information about your channel. Your channel (shop) can be managed by several users (with different emails) if you appoint someone to help you manage the Channel, we call it Multi Users 1 Channel. And vice versa, if you have several channels that can be managed by yourself (1 user), we call it 1 User Multi Channels. (Contact us, if you are interested in creating Multi Users 1 Channel or 1 User Multi Channels.)

Your Schedule is in Your Hand

Eatify's program was created for home chefs like you. Buyer (Buyer) orders/requests products no later than 1 day before the order is made and delivered (Pre Order). You can also arrange it yourself according to your capacity to make it easier for you to prepare materials, equipment, and even labor.

Focus to Your Strengths

Because we believe, time is a valuable thing. We make it easy for you regarding delivery. Eatify will arrange every delivery for all your orders placed through backtoeatify.com. You can use your time to hone your strengths by creating new products, new shapes and new flavors that can become an attraction for your culinary business.

What We Handle

Transactions

Entries via backtoeatify.com are always recorded in the Order Dashboard so that Chefs can clearly see what has happened and can double-check to avoid mistakes.

Delivery

Eatify has collaborated with delivery services. Chefs only need to carry out orders that have been ordered and record their name, complete address, number. The buyers' cellphones are recorded in the Order Dashboard for the order, so that the servers (deliverers) can clearly deliver your orders and we schedule delivery for all your orders that come in via backtoeatify.com. If an order comes in and you accept (tap approve) the order, the eatify system will immediately schedule a delivery service for each order you receive.

Product Statistic

You can see these statistics on each of your products. This is to help you analyze which products are most in demand by the public and which products you need to push to increase sales.
